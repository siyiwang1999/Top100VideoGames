Genre Visuals

The interactive graph is the genre distribution of Top 100 best-selling video games, which can help you get a preliminary overview of the major video game categories.

Looking at the number of each game genre in Top 100 video games alone does not directly lead to a relevant conclusion about whether that game genre is the most popular.

Therefore, the interactive chart below combines the sales and sales percentages of game genres as a way to help you gain a more comprehensive insight about the most popular video game genres. Sales percentage are based on the sales of that game genre divided by the total sales of Top 100 video games.
